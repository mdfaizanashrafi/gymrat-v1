# 🏋️ GymRat – Borderless Fitness Platform

> **Train Anywhere. Keep Every Streak.**

GymRat is a global fitness platform designed for people who refuse to let travel interrupt their fitness journey. Whether you're a digital nomad, business traveler, student, or athlete, GymRat helps you discover partner gyms, schedule sessions, track workouts, and maintain your streak anywhere in the world.

---

## 🌍 The Problem

Millions of fitness enthusiasts struggle to stay consistent when they:

* Travel for work
* Move to a new city
* Study abroad
* Become digital nomads
* Take extended vacations
* Frequently relocate

Changing locations often means:

❌ Losing workout routines
❌ Searching endlessly for quality gyms
❌ Breaking hard-earned fitness streaks
❌ Losing motivation and momentum

---

## 💡 The Solution

GymRat creates a **borderless fitness ecosystem**, allowing users to:

✅ Find verified gyms anywhere
✅ Book training sessions instantly
✅ Continue workout programs seamlessly
✅ Track progress across locations
✅ Join local fitness communities
✅ Protect and maintain fitness streaks

---

# ✨ Features

## 🗺️ Global Gym Locator

Discover partner gyms across cities and countries with advanced search and filtering.

## 📅 Session Scheduler

Book gym sessions and manage your training calendar from anywhere.

## 📊 Workout Log Sync

Store workout history in the cloud and continue your fitness journey seamlessly.

## 🔥 Streak Protection

Track consistency and protect your fitness streak while traveling.

## 👥 Community Features

Connect with local fitness enthusiasts and participate in challenges.

## 📈 Progress Dashboard

Monitor analytics, milestones, and long-term fitness performance.
